About Legacy of North

Legacy of North is a project that set out to preserve the vast history of Burnaby North Secondary School. Comprehensive 360 degree panoramic footage was gathered from over 300 locations and includes over 500 pieces of media to create a virtual tour of the historic buildings. The project also features other sources of footage that captures the history of the school from various contributors.
